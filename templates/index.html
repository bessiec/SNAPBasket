Our Vision

SNAPbasket is a community-based platform for SNAP recipients in the Los Angeles area to pool grocery price information and save money. Our goals are to provide:

  • SMS Service
  • Website
  • Mobile Accessible Website
  • Useful Information for SNAP Recipients

We are currently looking for smart partnerships to help move our vision forward.
