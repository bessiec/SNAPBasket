Meet Our Team

Bessie Chu Co-founder/Technical Lead

Bessie worked in Silicon Valley for five years in a ofvariety strategic and technical roles in the start-up, ad agency, and corporate worlds. She is currently pursuing a Master of Communication Management at the USC concentrating in New Communication Technologies and International Communication with a research focus on social media conversations and Big Data around sports advertisers and the World Cup.

Catie Peiper Co-founder/Partnerships

Catie is a first year Ph.D student at the USC School of Cinematic Arts in the Bryan Singer Division of Critical Studies. The inspiration for SNAPbasket began several years ago when the majority of Catie's monthly food budget depended on SNAP benefits; frustrated by the inequalities in local grocery pricing and the unpredictability of food inflation, she began compiling the pricing data for her local grocery stores that eventually became the early concept for SNAPbasket. Her goal, which is shared by the entire SNAPbaket team, is to make this data more easy collected and accessed, so that individuals working with limited budgets or who are dependent on SNAP benefits may be able to make the most of every dollar spent on food.Â 

Gabriel Shapiro Co-founder/Business Development

Gabriel is a second year student pursuing a Masters in Public Diplomacy at the USC. His research is primarily focused on building metrics for successful communication and developing research design to understand how individuals interact with technology on a daily basis. Prior to SNAPbasket Gabriel worked as a researcher at the Center for Communication, Leadership & Policy and as a media strategist and designer for the news agency Syria Direct.
